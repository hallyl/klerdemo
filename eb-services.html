EB1/EB2-NIW Service

We specialize in the EB1 and EB2-NIW petition processes, offering tailored strategies based on your profile, career objectives, and nationality. Whether you’re a professional on a temporary visa, a student, or a national from India or China, we work diligently to ensure your petition is optimized for approval. We guide you in preparing your petition and your USCIS forms to avoid errors with filing your forms and petition with the USCIS. We also provide personal coaching and guidance in building and improving your profile.

Navigating the immigration process can be challenging, but with Klerconsult, you are never alone.

Our clients are the most important people in our firm, and their success is our top priority. We understand that each client’s journey is unique, and we are dedicated to providing personalized, attentive service to meet their specific needs.

We provide end-to-end support, from initial assessment to document preparation and submission.

  • We offer expert guidance every step of the way
  • We are abreast with the most recent immigration information
